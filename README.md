# Strategic Product Placement Analysis: Unveiling Sales Impact with Tableau Visualization

## Project Overview
The **Strategic Product Placement Analysis** project aims to analyze how product positioning, pricing strategies, promotions, and consumer demographics influence sales performance in a retail environment.

Using **Tableau data visualization**, the project explores patterns and trends in retail data to understand how different factors affect customer purchasing behavior and overall product sales.

The objective of this project is to help businesses make **data-driven decisions** related to product placement, pricing strategies, and marketing efforts to improve sales performance.

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset used in this project contains retail sales information related to product categories, pricing, promotions, and customer demographics.

### Key Attributes in the Dataset

- Product Category  
- Product Position in Store  
- Sales Volume  
- Product Price  
- Competitor Price  
- Promotion Status  
- Consumer Demographics  
- Foot Traffic Levels  
- Seasonal Indicators  

This dataset enables the analysis of how different retail factors influence product performance and sales outcomes.

---

Before performing the analysis, the dataset was prepared and reviewed to ensure it was suitable for visualization.

### Data Preparation Steps

## Data Visualizations
Multiple visualizations were created using **Tableau** to analyze the impact of product placement and other factors on sales performance.

### 1. Average Sales Volume by Product Category
This visualization compares the average sales performance of different product categories such as **Clothing, Electronics, and Food**.

### 2. Competitor Price vs Product Price
This chart compares the **average competitor price with the product price** across categories to analyze pricing competitiveness.

### 3. Sales Volume by Product Category and Product Position
This visualization analyzes how **product placement within the store** (Front of Store, End-cap, Aisle) impacts sales performance across product categories.

### 4. Consumer Demographics vs Sales Volume
This chart shows how different customer groups such as **College Students, Families, Seniors, and Young Adults** contribute to total sales volume.

### 5. Product Category vs Price
This visualization highlights the **average product price across different categories** to understand pricing distribution.

### 6. Seasonal Impact on Sales
This analysis compares **sales performance during seasonal and non-seasonal periods** across product categories.

### 7. Foot Traffic vs Sales Volume
This visualization examines how **store foot traffic levels (High, Medium, Low)** combined with product positioning affect sales performance.

---

## Dashboard
All visualizations were combined into an interactive **Tableau dashboard** called **"Product Placement Analysis Dashboard"**.

The dashboard enables users to:

- Analyze product performance across categories  
- Compare product prices with competitor pricing  
- Understand the impact of promotions and seasons on sales  
- Evaluate how store foot traffic influences sales performance  
- Identify optimal product positioning strategies  

The dashboard provides a **comprehensive overview of product placement analysis in a retail environment**.

---

## Key Insights

- The **Clothing category** shows the highest average sales volume among the analyzed categories.  
- Products placed at the **front of the store** tend to generate higher sales.  
- **High foot traffic areas** significantly influence product sales performance.  
- **Consumer demographics** play an important role in determining purchasing behavior.
